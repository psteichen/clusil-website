Word from the President

As President of the CLUSIL, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our website.

With about 250 members and nearly 20 years of existence, the CLUSIL is a well established and recognized actor among the Information Security Landscape of Luxemburg and of the “Grande Region”.

CLUSIL will celebrate its 20th anniversary in 2016, in just 20 years we have come a very long way and are very proud.

The slogan of the CLUSIL is “by the experts for the experts”, meaning that all different professional experts concerned will find their added value by being member of the CLUSIL.

Thus my colleagues of the board and myself would like to invite all of you involved with information security to become member of the CLUSIL and to benefit from the exhaustive knowledge present, the possibility to exchange information among peers and to actively contribute to the promotion and enhancement of information security.

We hope by visiting regularly our WEB site it will inspire you to share our enthusiasm for information security.

Sincerely
Jean Goetzinger
President of CLUSIL

Objectives

  • To promote national information security activities, initiatives, and events
  • To promote CLUSIL activities (mainly WG results) at the national, European and International level
  • To have a neutral approach regarding any organization, particularly commercial companies
  • To favor CLUSIL members to have access to information security related events

Ethical charter

Rules regarding promotion, information and communication

  • Events of CLUSIL and its working groups
  • Events in which CLUSIL is a sponsor
  • Events of any other CLUSIx
  • Events organized by a non-commercial organization that is related to CLUSIL scope and topics of interest

Any other request for communication is carefully discussed within the Board and generally rejected if it is contrary to objectives of CLUSIL related to ethics and / or can harm the neutrality and integrity of CLUSIL with regards to the market.

Articles of Association

Created in 1996, under the denomination: CLUSSIL, Club de la Sécurité des Systèmes d'Information - Luxembourg.

Since 2012, and to adhere to current standards the name changed to CLUSIL, Club de la Sécurité de l'Information - Luxembourg.
