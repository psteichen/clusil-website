Become a member

CLUSIL members need to adhere and contribute to the association objectives, more details in About.

Private membership

To become a CLUSIL member as private person, the membership fee is: 100 EUR per year.

Company membership

For companies or other organisations (moral persons) there is a group membership fee of: 400 EUR per year. Giving you the possibility to subscribe 6 people.

For students a reduced fee is available on demand.
